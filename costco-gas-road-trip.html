Canada Border Crossing Checklist

Required Documents

  • Valid US Passport or Passport Card
  • Enhanced Driver's License (MI, NY, VT, MN, WA only)
  • Vehicle registration
  • Proof of auto insurance
  • For minors: birth certificate + parental consent letter if not traveling with both parents

Costco Gas Tips

  • US membership works at Canadian Costco
  • Canadian gas priced in CAD per litre
  • Visa credit/debit only — no cash
  • Gas station hours may differ from warehouse
  • Check Costco app or GasBuddy for live prices

Tolls to Expect

  • Ambassador Bridge: ~$5 USD
  • Peace Bridge: ~$6 USD
  • NY Thruway (I-90): Tolls apply
  • MA Turnpike (I-90): Tolls apply
  • Ontario highways: No tolls (except Hwy 407)

Planning Tips

  • Split into 2–3 driving days
  • Avoid border crossings on holiday weekends
  • Declare all purchases at US customs
  • Keep receipts from Canadian purchases
  • Download the ArriveCAN app (check if still required)

Distances and times are approximate. Verify Costco locations and hours before your trip at costco.com or costco.ca.
