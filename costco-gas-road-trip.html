Road Trip Essentials

Costco Gas Tips

  • Visa credit/debit or Costco card required — no cash
  • Gas station hours may differ from warehouse
  • Check Costco app or GasBuddy for live prices
  • Confirm Amherst, NY location is open before your trip (opening Spring 2026)

Tolls to Expect

  • Ohio Turnpike (I-80/I-90): Tolls apply
  • Pennsylvania stretch on I-90: Minimal/no toll
  • NY Thruway (I-90): Tolls apply
  • MA Turnpike (I-90): Tolls apply
  • Consider an E-ZPass for faster crossings

States You’ll Cross

  • Michigan — I-94 E, I-75 S
  • Ohio — Ohio Turnpike (I-80/I-90 E)
  • Pennsylvania — brief stretch on I-90 through Erie
  • New York — NY Thruway (I-90 E)
  • Massachusetts — Mass Pike (I-90 E) to Boston

Planning Tips

  • Split into 2–3 driving days for comfort
  • Syracuse/Rochester make good overnight stops
  • Watch for weather, especially snow belt near Erie
  • Keep snacks & water handy between stops
  • Bathroom break schedule every ~2 hours with kids
